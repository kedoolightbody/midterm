Synthetic Opioids Kill More People Yearly Than Heroin

In the year of 2016 Synthetic Opioids kill more people than Heroin in the US clocking in 19413 people while heroin kills 15469. Other drugs such as: Natural and Semisynthetic opioids and Methadone were also among the 4 highest killing streaks in regards to opioids in 2016.

Top ten opioid overdoses by state in USA. The data set shows that Ohio is number 1 with 3613 deaths in the year of 2016. The National Vital Statistics System multiple cause-of-death mortality files were used to identify drug overdose deaths. Drug overdose deaths were classified using the International Classification of Disease, Tenth Revision (ICD-10), based on the ICD-10 underlying cause-of-death codes X40–44 (unintentional), X60–64 (suicide), X85 (homicide), or Y10–Y14 (undetermined intent).
